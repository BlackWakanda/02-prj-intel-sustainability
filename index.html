Intel Sustainability Goals

Driving technology and innovation for a sustainable future — targets, milestones, and progress.

Roadmap & Milestones

1968

Intel Founded

Robert Noyce and Gordon Moore rename NM Electronics to Intel.

Foundation for decades of technological innovation and later sustainability efforts across operations and products.

1971

First Microprocessor

Intel debuts the 4004, ushering in the microprocessor era.

Microprocessors enabled vast efficiency gains in computing and later enabled innovations that support sustainability.

2006

Peak GHG Emissions

Highest operations emissions year, prompting action.

Intel invested in abatement, renewables, and efficiency to reduce emissions in subsequent years.

2020–2030

RISE & 2030 Goals

Responsible, Inclusive, Sustainable, Enabling strategy.

Ambitious goals for climate action, water stewardship, and waste reduction across the product lifecycle.

2022–2040

Net-Zero Roadmap

Commitment to net-zero emissions by 2040 for operations.

Combines clean energy sourcing, efficiency, and technology investments.
